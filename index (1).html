Why we built it

You know the look. The pencil goes down, the shoulders slump, and the page wins.

For a kid who learns differently, "just read the problem again" doesn't help. They need someone patient who slows down, shows the method, and makes the moment it clicks feel good — over and over, until it sticks. That's the whole job of Professor Paws.

How it teaches

A coach, not an answer key.

Professor Paws is built on how kids actually learn — seeing it, doing it with their hands, then proving they remember. Not memorizing, not guessing.

01

Snap the homework

Your child photographs the problem or types it in. No account for them to manage — you set everything up.

02

Walk the method

Professor Paws guides them through the how in small, calm steps — the same way a good tutor sitting beside them would.

03

Learn by doing

Hands-on activities — drag, build, and see the idea — instead of a wall of text. Made for kids who tune out of typing.

04

Make it stick

Skills come back at the right time, so what clicked today is still there next week — real learning, not a one-time win.
