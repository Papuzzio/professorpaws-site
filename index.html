Questions parents ask

Does it give children the answer?

No. It guides the method in small steps — visuals, manipulatives, hints — and breaks a step down smaller when your child is stuck, instead of revealing the answer.

What does the AI actually do?

The AI writes the lesson — pacing, wording, encouragement. The maths is checked by code, not by the AI, and every phonics letter sound is real recorded human speech. There's no open-ended AI chat anywhere in the app.

What data do you collect about my child?

The minimum a learning coach needs: a parent email, a first name or nickname, and learning progress. Handwriting stays on the device. Nothing is sold, shared for ads, or used to train AI. Details in our Privacy Policy.

What ages and subjects?

Children roughly 8–13, met at the level they're actually working at. The beta covers spellings and phonics, times tables and number work, telling time, handwriting, and home reading.

Is a diagnosis required?

No. It's for children who learn differently or regularly find homework overwhelming — with or without a formal diagnosis.

Can my child make purchases?

No. The parent controls the account and any subscription. There is nothing a child can buy.

What device, and what will it cost?

Designed for iPad. Free during the private beta; a paid subscription at public launch — beta families will always see pricing before paying anything.

Does it replace school or tutoring?

No — it supports the work your child is already learning, and makes home practice calmer and more accessible.
